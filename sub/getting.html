Car Hire Clark:
Avis + others. Similar to Manila.
Bus
If you wish to take public transport try the Five Star and Victory Liner bus companies. These travel from Manila to Bolinao either from Pasay bus station near the airport or Cubao (Edsa). The fare is about p300 for an non - A/C bus and p400 for A/C bus
