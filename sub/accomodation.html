Accomodation

The resort has a range of accommodation, namely small and large sized cabanas with fans; and air-conditioned bedrooms rooms with cable TV and DVD facilities including : Riviera bedrooms ( 2 pax); Sunset bedrooms (4), La Marea and La Breeza bedrooms (4), Villa Del Mar 4 (6), On Suite 1 (6),Familia 8 (8) and 14 (8); and El GrandeJnr (10) and El Grande (15) bedrooms. Villa Del Mar, Familia 14 and El Grande have some self catering facilities. For list of full bedrooms facilities, hot and cold showers, sport and site amenities see our Tariff

Built with fresh sea breezes, views and spatial integration in mind our terraced balcony buildings Alfa and Beta surround our large central garden.
