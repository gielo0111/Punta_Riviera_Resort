Resort Surroundings

As shown in the slideshow opposite the resort grounds are located on the banks on the River Malino just next to its estuary with the South China Sea with a unique environmental land and marine ecology. The grounds are linked with the adjacent sandbar and long peninsular by bridge and the river runs parallel with the sea for almost 2kms.

The river can be explored in land by the adventurous for about 3kms upstream through our nippa and mangrove fringed Amazonian like terrain which is good for bird watching. Good walks for about 1 hr can be made along the beach in either direction and returning through the villages or coconut plantations. The coral fringe is rich with shells and sea creatures however the waves although beautiful can be formidable. Nearby there are sets of caves for underground swimming. Many rock formations and inlets can be explored and visits made to patar white beach, the lighthouse and bolinao falls with snorkeling to see the Giant clams at Santiago Island, balingasay and Bolinao have a range of other tourist attractions.
