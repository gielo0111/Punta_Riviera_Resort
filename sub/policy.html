House Rules

House Rules

1) The maximum number of persons in each room must not exceed the number specified at time of booking in.
2) The following are not allowed in any of the resort rooms:
  • Smoking
  • Pets, animals, prohibited drugs, firearms or weapons of any sort
  • Cooking facilities except at designated areas
  • Electrical appliances such us irons, electrical kettles or heaters.
  • Music centers
3)Unregistered guests
  • Drivers and maid are to be registered as guests. Please ask for available accommodation and special rates.
  • Guests are required to avoid excess noise that may disturb other guests.
4) Alcohol, drinks and food brought on the resort are subject to the following:
  • Corkage charges e.g 500 Pesos per bottle of Alcohol spirits.
  • A service fee for use of the resort utilities.
  • Please declare any such Beverages at Reception before consumption on site
5) The removal or lost of any room accessories including towels, bedding, TV and DVD player shall be the liability of the guest.

6) Friends of the guest should be booked in at the Reception as Day Trippers at the appropriate rate (8:30a.m- 6:30p.m. only).

7) Guests are reminded that the resort is located in the countryside and appropriate protection eg footwear is recommended. A doctor is resident at Bolinao.

8) Advance Booking: A non returnable deposit of 50% of room rate is required. This may be reduced to 30% in special circumstances at the sole discretion of the owners.

THE RESORT IS NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR LOSS OR THEFT OF GUESTS' PERSONAL PROPERTY WHILST IN THE RESORT.

Please declare high cost items if you expect any special security measures to be taken by us. Please look after your valuables, lock them in your luggage, your car, the cabinets provided or leave them in the care of our reception. The Resort disowns liability for any violation of the Laws of the Republic of the Philippines made by guests whilst Inside the Resort
