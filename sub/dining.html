Dining

Aileen's Punta Riviera speialties have been featured on Channel 4'Out of Town' and include all types of seafood such as milk fish, durado and giant tuna with mouthwatering sauces; Chinese chili crabs and tiger pawns, Shanghai spring rolls; Thai salads; and Pinoy specialties such as dinakdakan, lechon(whole roasted pig); chicken adobo, grilled pork chops and BBQ galore; and shame on you if you miss an Aileenoburger afore you go!

The white burgundy will go very well with your tuna Madam. However may we recommend a bottle of Kangaroo Ridge Shiraz or perhaps the Wolf Blass Yellow Label Cab to go with your steak sir? Share a giggle, laugh, a glass of quality wine, good food and evenings entertainment at our Wine Bistro with your proprietors Ian and Aileen to complete another happy memorable holiday at the resort.
