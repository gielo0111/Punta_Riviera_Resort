Travel Guide



 Safety Issues and Guarding your Property
  • Please note that we do not, under any circumstances, accept responsibility for loss or theft of your valuables.
  • Check it the weather first and the conditions outside the harbour.
  • Don't be fooled by cheap fares - the cheaper they are the more you may be risking your life.
  • Ask about the availability of lifejackets - if not available at least have your kids carry their poolside floats
  • Our tap water is clean enough for brushing your teeth but don't drink it.
  • Watch out for infrequent falling coconuts and branches from our buko trees when it is windy.
  • Please observe our swimming pool rules.
  • Please don't forget to bring your regular swimsuit and sun tan oil/block and a hat. The heat in summer months is awesome and shade is a valuable commodity
  • If you have a medical condition, allergies or special dietary needs please bring along your own medicine or facilities.
  • Please also advise us in advance of your arrival, when booking in at reception and when ordering meals as appropriate if these needs or dietary conditions are severe.
  • Please don't be shy about approaching us at any time on these and other important issues. Our site is suitable for wheelchairs.
  • Cars and vans can be rented at most airports by companies.
 TRAVELLING
  • Note that you may need to pay a cash deposit and to show your air ticket, three forms of I.D. and international driver's license.
  • If you wish to take public transport try the Five Star and Victory Liner Bus. These travel from Manila toBolinao either from Pasay bus station near the airport or Cubao (Edsa). The fare is about p300 for an non - A/C bus and p400 for A/C bus.
  • Arrive at Manila Airport at least three hours in advance and be aware that the traffic in and around Manila can be busy.
  • We now accept major four major credit cards for payment of up to 50% your bill and, for confirmed booking, prefer an Advance Payment by telegraphic transfer to our PNB account for 50% of your room rate
