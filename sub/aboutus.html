About Us

About Us

Punta Riviera Del Mar Resort is an upmarket, romantic family resort located in the heart of Luzon Island to the North of Manila facing westwards towards the South China Sea. It is reached by road. For overseas guests visiting Philippines for the first time we recommend car + driver hire from one of the reputable firms at Manila or Clark Airports (see Transport) or by bus. We also recommend a review of our Travel Guide (Practical Tips for Surviving the Philippines) that deals with important issues like local customs, safety, security and financial matters

Where the River Ilog Malino meets the sea on this stunning coastline each morning brings a touch of magic to our doorstep here at Punta Riviera Resort,Brgy,Ilog Malino, Bolinao, Pangasinan, Luzon,Philippines. Join us and share a peaceful and meaningful holiday in harmony with nature. Shed your frustrations and city stresses as cool sea breezes immerses your body in fresh air and lift your spirits, whilst sand and seawater runs between your toes.

Morning rush-hour traffic as viewed from your balcony is guaranteed to be both light and tolerable. Back to nature - paddle one of our kayaks for several kilometers up the mangrove fringed Amazonian-like Ilog Malino lagoon - but watch out for iguanas!

Uniquely located at the estuary of the IlogMalino river the view from our elevated sea frontage embraces a stunning marine ecology with constant, natural air conditioning from incoming cool sea breezes as frothy turquoise waves crash onto the edge of the coral fringe only 100m away. To our right a deep mangrove fringed river can be seen where frogs, crabs and iguanas thrive and bangus are harvested.

Such natural environments are forever changing as tides come and go, the river rises, falls and twists and shade from our coconut trees (boco) create a moving visual feast of textured patterns on our lawns. No wonder why so many Filipina girls chose to have their beach wedding at Punta!

In 2011 our new Don Quixote bar (above, below ) was opened by the riverside next to our new Spa facilities offering indoor pursuits such as pool, darts, karaoke, table tennis in addition to a range of board games,WiFi facilities, cable TV, and video and book libraries. Outdoor pastimes and games include volleyball, kayaking, beach games, swimming, snorkeling, fishing, beach combing, walking and jogging. A range of Team Building, artistic and cultural pursuits, aerobics and live bands can be arranged for groups with advance notice. Bonfires, fireworks and beach BBQ facilities are also available under the stars on our canderoma fringed island.

The resort owners Dr Ian and Aileen McFeat-Smith (above left) aim to provide a safe holiday for you with a blend of local and international flavor. We are a family resort that caters for both fun and relaxation for all members of your group. The resort has been in full operation since 2006 and whilst being relatively new is well experienced in hotel management operation with a Team of 30-40 trained staff that are retained all year round irrespective of market conditions. Our BLSTLC school activities compliments our constant need for training, individual development and improving the range of skills needed by our staff to meet your diverging needs and expectations. The resort has been built and is being developed with security, sustainability and environmental integration in mind. In addition to safety and fun amenities, providing high quality food at affordable prices in the provinces remains our constant challenge. Aileen meets this task with an unusual level of vigor and enthusiasm and we are focused on reducing the time for delivery of hot meals. As a guest you need to shed your big city and travel frustrations and we aim to help you achieve this. So come on down and experience the magic and ambience of this unique beach / countryside resort with some of the kindest and happiest people to be found in the world.
